About me

My name is Natalia Madrid, and I chose to study engineering driven by my passion for solving complex problems. When I discovered that climate change poses one of the greatest threats to humanity and our planet, I felt a deep calling to be part of the solution. I believe that purpose-driven dreams can change the world. Now, as a proud Fulbright scholar and a Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of California, San Diego, I am committed to advancing climate change adaptation and mitigation.


Please, feel free to reach me at namadrid@ucsd.edu. I would love to exchange ideas, and explore future collaborations!
