About me

I am an Industrial Engineer and Ph.D. student in Economics at UC San Diego, passionate about addressing complex social challenges, particularly climate change. As a Fulbright scholar, my research focuses on the intersection of environmental economics and macro-finance, aiming to advance solutions for climate adaptation and mitigation.


I have received several prestigious recognitions, including the Fulbright Scholarship, the Outstanding Woman Engineer Award from the University of Concepción, and a Chilean Ministry of Education scholarship for academic excellence.


Feel free to reach me at namadrid@ucsd.edu—I’d love to connect, share ideas, and explore collaborations!
