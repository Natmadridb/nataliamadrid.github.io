About me

My name is Natalia Madrid. I decided to study engineering because I am passionate about solving complex problems. When I learned about the severe threat of climate change to humanity and our planet, I knew I had to be part of the solution. I believe that having a strong purpose can change the world. Currently, I am a Fulbright scholar and a Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of California, San Diego. I am focused on making progress in adapting to and mitigating the impacts of climate change.


Please, feel free to reach me at namadrid@ucsd.edu. I would love to exchange ideas, learn about your projects, and explore future collaborations!
