About me

Hi, my name is Natalia Madrid. I decided to study engineering, driven by my curiosity and the search for solutions to complex problems. When I understood that climate change is one of the most significant threats facing humanity and the planet, I knew I wanted to be part of the solution. I am convinced that dreams with purpose come true. Thanks to a Fulbright scholarship, I am currently a PhD student at the University of California, San Diego.
